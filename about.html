About Chef Sali

Transforming Indian Culinary Excellence into Global Business Success

From Kitchen to Consultant

What began as a passion for authentic Indian flavors evolved into a professional journey dedicated to mastering both culinary craft and restaurant management.

With over 7+ years of experience in kitchen operations, menu engineering, cost control, and team leadership, Chef Sali developed a unique consulting approach that blends culinary excellence with strategic business thinking.

This vision led to the creation of Cuplana Consulting — a brand built to transform restaurants into profitable, scalable, and operationally efficient food businesses.
